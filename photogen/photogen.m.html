In the U.S. Army Reserve, you serve part-time, which means you can continue to pursue your goals while staying close to home; and making a difference to our Army and our Nation.

The #ArmyReserveChallenge is a challenge that matters -- to our Nation and, potentially, to your own future.

To take the #ArmyReserveChallenge:
1. Visit your local recruiter, have a conversation, and take a photo.
2. Upload a photo of yourself and your local recruiter here.
3. Share it to your feed with #ArmyReserveChallenge

Three simple steps and you may be featured across our goarmy platforms!
